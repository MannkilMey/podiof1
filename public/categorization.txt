WHAT IS PODIOF1?
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

PodioF1 is a FREE Formula 1 sports prediction platform where 
users compete with friends by predicting race results.

Similar to:
- ESPN Fantasy Sports
- Yahoo Fantasy Sports
- NFL Fantasy Football
- FanDuel Fantasy (free version)

Purpose:
Entertainment and friendly competition among Formula 1 fans.

───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
HOW IT WORKS
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

1. Users create a free account
2. Join or create a private group with friends
3. Before each F1 race, predict the top 10 finishers
4. Earn points for accurate predictions
5. Compete in a private leaderboard with friends

No money is involved at any point.
